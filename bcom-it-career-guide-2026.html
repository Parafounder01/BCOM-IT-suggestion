Practical Roadmap — 2026

Month-by-month action plan for B.Com (IT) graduates to maximise offers and exam outcomes.

Immediate — June & July
Apply for Mass Recruitment & Bank Exams
Applications open: TCS BPS (NQT), Infosys BPM, Cognizant CCAT. Register for IBPS PO and SBI PO before deadlines. Prepare basic quantitative aptitude and reasoning for online tests.
TCS BPS Infosys BPM Cognizant CCAT IBPS PO SBI PO
Skill Building — June to August
12-Week Technical Upskill Sprint
Weeks 1–2: SQL fundamentals — joins, aggregations, subqueries.
Weeks 3–4: Advanced Excel — pivot tables, lookups, macros.
Weeks 5–8: Power BI — data modelling, DAX, dashboards.
Weeks 9–12: Python — pandas, matplotlib, automation scripts.
SQL Excel Power BI Python
Ongoing — June to September
Government Exam Preparation
SBI PO / IBPS PO: Dedicate 2 hours daily to reasoning, quantitative aptitude, English, and general awareness. Take weekly mock tests.
SSC CGL Tier-I: Exam window August–September. Focus on quantitative aptitude, general intelligence, English, and general awareness.
SBI PO Prep IBPS PO Prep SSC CGL Tier-I Mock Tests
